Caramel Mocha Latte
(Recipe)

1.Mix together the turbinado sugar and sea salt. Set aside.
2.Heat the milk, 2 Tbsp. caramel, hot chocolate mix (or chocolate syrup), and pinch sea salt until just below boiling. Pour into a blender and pulse until frothy. Pour into a cup. Slowly pour the hot coffee into one side of the frothed milk. Top with Cool Whip.
