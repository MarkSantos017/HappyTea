Green Tea Bubble
(Recipe)



1.1½ teaspoons matcha (green tea powder)
2.2 tablespoons hot water.
3.2 tablespoons acacia honey (adjust the amount to your liking)
4.1 cup milk.
5.½ cup ice cubes.
6.2 scoops green tea flavored ice cream, optional.
7.¼ cup cooked giant black tapioca pearls, if you make bubble tea.
