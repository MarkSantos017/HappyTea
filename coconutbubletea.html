Coconut Bubble Tea
(Recipe)


1.In a cup add the tapioca and syrup mixture.
2.And in a shaker, add ⅔ cup of ice cubes, ¼ cup of coconut cream (4 tablespoons), ½ cup of cold milk, and 1 tablespoon of simple syrup. And shake
3.rigorously until combine. Then pour over the tapioca-syrup mixture.
