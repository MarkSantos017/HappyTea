Frosted Coffee
(Recipe)


1.Coarsely grind coffee beans.
2.Place ground coffee in large container with water and allow to steep in refrigerator overnight. 3.Strain through cheese cloth of filter to remove beans.
4.Add one cup of coffee to two cups of ice cream in blender.
5.Run until consistency of milk shake. Repeat for other serving.
